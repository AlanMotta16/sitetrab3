GET TO KNOW YOUR PC COMPONENTS

Do you not know anything about PCs? Here you'll find enough information to get you going, and them some!

Component Name Company that Makes Them Price Range (not 100% accurate) Component Description Examples
CPU AMD - Intel R$200-R$20.000 Commonly known as the "Brain of the PC". Stands for 'Central Processing Unit', and processes everything the PC does.
Graphics Card MSI - Gigabyte - ASUS - EVGA - Galax - NVIDIA R$500-R$20.000 Also called a GPU (Graphics Processing Unit). It's similar to a CPU, except it processes images and graphics. Mostly used for videogames.
Motherboard ASUS - ASRock - Gigabyte R$500-R$20.000 Connects all the other components together, literally. Is a relatively flat surface, and connects electricity to the PC via the power supply.
RAM Kingston - Corsair R$100-R$5.000 Type of memory for instant use (like Chrome browsers). Stands for 'Random Acess Memory'.
Memory Drive Kingston - Corsair - Samsung R$200-R$5.000 Memory for normal use. Stores all of your files, and operates downloads and uploads. Usually have bigger capacity than RAM memory.
Case Literally too many to list. R$20-R$5.000 The box where all of the components fit into. Can be bought at multiple sizes, and ordered to custom designs.
Power Supply EVGA - ASUS R$500+ VERY IMPORTANT TO BUY HIGH QUALITY!!! Feeds electrical power to your PC, can catch on fire if not made properly.
